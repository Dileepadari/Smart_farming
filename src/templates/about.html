GREEN PLANT


INTRO TO WEBSITE

GREEN PLANT is a user-friendly website designed to provide real-time information on plant conditions. The website's intuitive user interface offers a seamless experience for plant enthusiasts, gardeners, and indoor growers.This website shows us the current conditions of the plants by displaying the data outputted by sensors.

In this website, there are several webpages which we can see on the side navigation bar.Home page displays the real time data of all sensors in graph form.Statistics also contain the graph data.Analysis page shows what are the limits of every sensor for which the plant grows safely.Alerts webpage is the place where any alerts or warnings are displayed indicating that sensor value is off limit i.e; the plant is stressed.Also there is Settings where you can change some settings of the website and customise it.


Uses of the website

Our website is a valuable tool for plant enthusiasts and caretakers, offering the following benefits:

Optimized Plant Care: Users can tailor their care routines to each plant's unique needs by constantly monitoring and adjusting humidity, soil moisture, light, and air quality.

Remote Monitoring: Accessible from any device with an internet connection, this website enables users to monitor their plants from anywhere, making it ideal for frequent travelers

Data-Driven Decisions: Historical data and alerts empower users to make informed decisions about plant care, helping them prevent issues and promote plant growth.
